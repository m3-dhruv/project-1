Yuvarangat 2022

The Garba Mahotsav was held on the huge Football ground of the main campus where an auspicious idol of Maa Amba was present. As per the tradition, the Aarti event started at around 6:30 PM followed by the Garba. The majority of students showed enthusiasm and participated in the Garba with their friends. After enjoying the Garba, it was time to have snacks at the food stalls present on the ground.

Youth Festival

This is the 22nd National Youth Festival. The 1st National Youth Festival was held in 1995 in Bhopal. The Theme of the Festival is 'Sankalp Se Siddhi', to capitalize on the demographic dividend of young India, to capture the vibrancy and fresh perspective of youth and to pledge to accomplish the Goal of New India.
